recipe for khichadi by Akanksha

Ingedients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow lentils(moong dal)
  • 1 onion,finely chopped
  • 1 tomato,chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • salt to taste
  • water

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and lentils together under running water.
  2. In a pressure coocker, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and let them aplutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and fry them until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add termeric powder,rice,lentils, and salt. Mix well.
  6. pour in water(about 4 cup) and close the pressure coocker lid.
  7. Cook for about 3-4 whistles or until rice and lentils are well-cooked.
  8. Once the pressure released, open the cooker and give the khichadi a gentle stir.

Serve hot with yogurt,pickel,or any side of your choice
